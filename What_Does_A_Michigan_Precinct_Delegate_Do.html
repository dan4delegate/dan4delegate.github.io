Represent Your Neighborhood

A precinct delegate is an elected official who represents their neighborhood (precinct) within the political party.

Connect with Voters

They help communicate the concerns and needs of their neighbors to party leaders. Precinct delegates also inform and engage voters about important issues and upcoming elections.

Support Candidates

They work to support and promote the party’s candidates during elections. Precinct delegates might help with campaigns by distributing information, making phone calls, and encouraging people to vote.

Attend Conventions

They attend county conventions to help choose party leaders and sometimes delegates to higher-level conventions (like state or national conventions). At these conventions, precinct delegates help shape the party’s platform and policies.

Vote on Party Leadership

Precinct delegates vote for party leadership positions, which helps determine who will lead the party at the county level.

Why It Matters

Voice in the Party: Your precinct delegate gives you a voice in the direction and decisions of the political party.

Strengthen Community: It helps ensure that your community's needs and preferences are heard and addressed by party leaders.

Promote Good Governance: Precinct delegates contribute to the democratic process by supporting candidates who align with the values and interests of their community.
